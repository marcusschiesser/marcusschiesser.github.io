If your using Jersey as JAX-RS implementation your lucky: Just add to the <servlet> tag in your web.xml the following snippet:

<init-param>
	<param-name>com.sun.jersey.api.json.POJOMappingFeature</param-name>
	<param-value>true</param-value>
</init-param>

After restarting your servlet, your POJOs are marshalled to JSON as a charme. Enjoy!
